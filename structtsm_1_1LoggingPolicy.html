Detailed Description

template<typename StateType>
struct tsm::LoggingPolicy< StateType >

A simple and Generic LoggingPolicy. When inserted between say AsyncExecutionPolicy and e.g. Hsm<Switch> to look like using AsyncLoggingSwitch = AsyncExecutionPolicy<LoggingPolicy<Switch>>>, entry, exit and transitions can be logged. Pair with your favorite LOG macros.
